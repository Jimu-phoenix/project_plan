========================================================
BUSINESS FINANCIAL REPUTATION SYSTEM
========================================================

VISION
--------------------------------------------------------
Transform the platform from a simple credit scoring app into
Malawi's Financial Reputation Infrastructure   a trusted digital
reputation layer that lets SMEs prove financial credibility to
banks, MFIs, SACCOs, mobile money lenders, suppliers, insurers,
and investors using verifiable financial evidence rather than
collateral alone.

This does not replace the Credit Reference Bureau (CRB). It
complements it by building financial identities for businesses
that currently have little or no formal credit history.


CORE OBJECTIVES
--------------------------------------------------------
- Help informal businesses become bankable
- Reduce lending risk for financial institutions
- Improve financial inclusion
- Increase transparency between businesses and lenders
- Encourage good financial behaviour through measurable reputation growth
- Create a portable business reputation that follows the business
  across different financial institutions

========================================================
2. PROCESSING (AI ENGINE)
========================================================

Rather than producing a single score, the AI builds a complete
financial profile by analysing:

- Cash flow stability
- Revenue consistency and growth trends
- Payment discipline
- Savings behaviour
- Loan repayment behaviour
- Trade references (supplier/customer)
- Financial resilience
- Fraud indicators
- Seasonal business patterns
- Business maturity
- Digital adoption
- Customer and supplier reliability

--------------------------------------------------------
2.1 Explainable AI
--------------------------------------------------------
The AI never produces an unexplained decision.

Instead of:
    "Loan Approved"

It explains:

    Approved because:
    - Business has operated continuously for four years
    - Utility payments are consistently made on time
    - Monthly revenue has increased by 18%
    - Suppliers rate the business highly
    - No suspicious transaction behaviour detected

    Risk flagged:
    - Seasonal revenue reduction expected in January

--------------------------------------------------------
2.2 Business Trust Index (Multi-Category Scoring)
--------------------------------------------------------
Instead of one overall score, display individual category scores:

- Financial Stability
- Cash Flow Strength
- Revenue Growth
- Payment Discipline
- Savings Behaviour
- Business Longevity
- Supplier Trust
- Customer Trust
- Digital Adoption
- Fraud Risk
- Business Maturity

--------------------------------------------------------
2.3 Reputation Timeline
--------------------------------------------------------
Tracks historical reputation growth over time, e.g.:

    2023 -> 620
    2024 -> 710
    2025 -> 810
    2026 -> 845

Lets lenders see whether a business is improving or declining.

--------------------------------------------------------
2.4 Reputation Events
--------------------------------------------------------
Every financial action affects the score, e.g.:

- Utility bill paid
- Supplier payment completed
- Customer payment received
- Loan repaid early
- Business licence renewed
- Tax return submitted
- Monthly savings goal achieved
- Business milestone reached

--------------------------------------------------------
2.5 Reputation Growth Coaching
--------------------------------------------------------
The system teaches businesses how to improve their score, e.g.:

    Your score decreased by 18 points because utility
    payments were delayed.

    Recommendations:
    - Enable automatic bill payments
    - Increase monthly savings
    - Reduce unnecessary expenses
    - Improve supplier payment consistency

    Expected improvement: +30 points within three months

--------------------------------------------------------
2.6 Financial Reputation Simulator
--------------------------------------------------------
Businesses can simulate future decisions, e.g.:

- "What happens if I apply for a MWK 10 million loan?"
- "What happens if I hire five more employees?"
- "What happens if revenue decreases by 20%?"
- "What happens if I increase monthly savings?"

The AI predicts the effect on:
- Reputation score
- Loan eligibility
- Financial risk
- Cash flow
- Business growth

--------------------------------------------------------
2.7 Business Financial Twin
--------------------------------------------------------
A digital "twin" of the business that predicts future (not just
current) performance:

- Future cash flow
- Future reputation
- Loan repayment capability
- Business growth
- Financial risks
- Seasonal challenges
- Expansion readiness

--------------------------------------------------------
2.8 Trade Reference Network
--------------------------------------------------------
Businesses request verified references from suppliers, customers,
and partners. Each reference evaluates:

- Payment reliability
- Product quality
- Communication
- Trustworthiness
- Business relationship

Reference weight scales with the reputation of the person giving
it   trusted businesses create stronger references than unknown ones.

--------------------------------------------------------
2.9 Business Verification Levels (Badges)
--------------------------------------------------------
    Bronze    - Business Exists
    Silver    - Business Verified
    Gold      - Financial Records Verified
    Platinum  - Trusted by Financial Institutions
    Diamond   - Outstanding Financial Reputation

--------------------------------------------------------
2.10 Reputation Decay
--------------------------------------------------------
Reputation is not static. If a business becomes inactive or stops
showing good financial behaviour, its score gradually declines.
Recent behaviour carries more weight than older behaviour.

--------------------------------------------------------
2.11 Fraud Detection
--------------------------------------------------------
The AI continuously monitors for unusual behaviour, e.g.:

- Sudden unexplained revenue spikes
- Fake transaction patterns
- Circular payments
- Multiple businesses using identical information
- Suspicious account activity
- Rapid changes in transaction behaviour

Flagged cases are sent for lender review.

--------------------------------------------------------
2.12 Industry Benchmarking
--------------------------------------------------------
Compares a business against similar businesses, e.g.:

    Your Business Trust Index:      845
    Average Grocery Store:          720
    Top 10%:                        905

--------------------------------------------------------
2.13 Confidence Score
--------------------------------------------------------
Every AI prediction includes a confidence level, e.g.:

    Business Trust Index:        845
    Prediction Confidence:       96%
    Data Sources Used:           12 Verified Sources

--------------------------------------------------------
2.14 Loan Readiness Assessment
--------------------------------------------------------
Instead of a binary Eligible / Not Eligible, show readiness, e.g.:

    Current Loan Readiness: 82%

    Requirements to reach Premium Loan Tier:
    - Increase monthly savings
    - Maintain timely utility payments for three more months
    - Obtain two additional verified supplier references

6. PRIVACY AND CONSENT
========================================================

Every data source requires explicit consent from the business
owner. The business owner can:

- View all connected data sources
- Grant or revoke access
- See who accessed their profile
- Challenge incorrect information
- Download their reputation report


========================================================
7. LONG-TERM VISION
========================================================

The platform evolves into Malawi's national Business Financial
Infrastructure   a trusted reputation layer used by banks, MFIs,
mobile money providers, insurers, suppliers, investors, and
government agencies.

Instead of businesses repeatedly proving their credibility to
every institution, they build one portable, continuously updated
Financial Reputation that grows with their business and unlocks
access to finance, partnerships, and new opportunities.

This positions the platform as foundational digital infrastructure
for financial inclusion   not just another loan-scoring application.
